Hello again Reader,

This month we crossed 40,000 typed verses, which means we have collectively typed the Bible once and are already a third of the way through again. That blows my mind.

To celebrate, I have been working on some new features.

## August Updates

- Refreshed the "Log" section of [/history](https://www.typetheword.site/history) to better visualize typing consistency.
- Added a new settings menu in the auth dropdown with light/dark theme switching.
- Added keyboard shortcuts:
    - ctrl/cmd + shift + Y -> [/history](https://www.typetheword.site/history)
    - ctrl/cmd + shift + , -> opens Settings
    - ctrl/cmd + backspace -> restart verse
    - alt/option + backspace -> delete previous word
