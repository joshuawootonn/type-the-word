## September Updates

### A New Home Page

When I was creating TTW, I wanted new users to immediately be able to try the product without signing up, so the home page originally directed straight to Psalm 23.

I now think that made onboarding and understanding the product harder, and from an SEO perspective it prevented TTW from ranking.

In this home page refresh, I added more contextual details for new users explaining what this site is about, while still giving them an immediate chance to type.

### Fixes

The keyboard shortcuts introduced in August did not work for Windows / Linux. Those are now fixed:

- ctrl + shift + Y -> [/history](https://www.typetheword.site/history)
- ctrl + shift + , -> opens Settings
- ctrl + shift + backspace -> restart verse
- ctrl + backspace -> delete previous word

## A scripture I have been thinking about

Joshua 24:14-15 and Philippians 3:13-14 were both on my mind this month.

The phrase "choose this day whom you will serve" stood out to me as a strong call to action. God's grace is new this morning. Forget what lies behind, choose Christ today, and strain forward in honoring him.
