December 2025 Type the Word

Hi Reader,

Since August, total verses typed grew from 310,000 to 480,000. November had a record 220 signups, and December was already on track to be the top month yet.

## WPM / Accuracy Stats (Beta)

An early-access WPM and accuracy feature is now available via settings and shown in `/history`.

This was a long-requested feature. The longer-term direction is to keep it optional so scripture focus remains central for those who prefer it.

## New Translations (Alpha)

A super-early-access translation selector launched with:

- NIV
- NLT
- CSB
- NKJV
- NASB
- MSG
- BSB
- NTV

This was enabled through [api.bible](https://api.bible/). The release was explicitly alpha with known formatting and attribution gaps still being addressed.
