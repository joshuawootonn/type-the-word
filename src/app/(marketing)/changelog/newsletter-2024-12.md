December 2024 Type the Word

Hello again Reader,

Since the October email, users have almost typed an entire Bible worth of verses, and 97,446 verses were typed in 2024.

## December Updates

Following up from October priorities:

1. More translations
2. Custom themes
3. Speed and accuracy stats

### Custom Themes

Custom themes are now live in settings. You can create themes, choose separate light/dark themes under "System", and experiment with creative combinations.

The goal was to address accessibility issues with red/green defaults while also adding fun personalization.

### Fixes

Worked on incorrect spacing in the quote within [Matthew 6:10-13](https://www.typetheword.site/passage/matthew_6). The issue was improved but not fully resolved at the time of this update.

## What's next?

Plan for the rest of December: fix several user-reported issues.

Plan for 2025: continue smaller fixes and later work on speed/accuracy and more translations.
