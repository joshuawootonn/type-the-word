October 2024 Type the Word

Hey there Reader,

This month, we crossed 70,000 verses typed on TTW, which means we have typed the Bible over two times.

## October Updates

In October, I mainly fixed bugs and interacted with users to figure out what is next for TTW. Three feature requests were top of mind:

1. More translations like NLT and CSB
2. Custom color themes
3. Speed and accuracy statistics

### Speed and Accuracy

This has been the most requested feature by far. I was initially hesitant because I did not want to distract from scripture focus, but I think this can be solved by making the experience configurable.

### Custom Color Themes

In September someone reached out about red/green colors being hard to distinguish. Custom themes should improve accessibility and add flexibility.

### More Translations

I reached out to Lifeway for CSB access and am also exploring NLT.

### Fixes

- Removed flicker on the left-side completion line when you complete a verse.
- Fixed Song of Solomon formatting so chapters 4 through 6 are accessible.

Until next time.
