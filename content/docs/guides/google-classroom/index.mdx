## What this integration does

Type the Word integrates with Google Classroom so teachers can create typing assignments from passages and students can complete them in the app.

At a high level:

- Admins enable and approve the Google Workspace app configuration.
- Teachers connect their Google account and create assignments.
- Students connect their account, complete assignments, and turn them in.

## Who these guides are for

- **Workspace Admins**: setup and approval in Google Cloud / Workspace
- **Teachers**: account connection, assignment creation, and progress tracking
- **Students**: account connection and assignment completion workflow

## Recommended setup flow

1. Enable Google Workspace integration.
2. Connect a teacher account.
3. Create and publish an assignment.
4. Connect a student account.
5. Complete and turn in assignments.
